Extending the benefits under Agrahara Insurance Scheme, which is 

implemented for the retired Public Officers  

 
Your  attention  is  hereby  drawn  to  the  provisions  in  Public  Administration  Circular 
12/2005(VIII) dated 29.10.2021. 
 
02.  
The above circular has been issued  with the objective of providing special relief  for 
the  pensioners  retired  before  01.01.2016  as  well  as  pensioners  above  the  age  of  70  years. 
However, in considering various representations/ objections in this regard, implementation of 
the provisions of the said circular is hereby suspended until an appropriate decision is made 
after discussing again with all the relevant parties.
