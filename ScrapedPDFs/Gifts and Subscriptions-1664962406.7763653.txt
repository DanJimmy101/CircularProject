Gifts and Subscriptions 

 

Your attention is hereby drawn to Sub Section 3, Chapter XLVII, Volume II of Establishments 

Code.  

 

02. 

In  terms  of  the  provisions  of  the  aforesaid  Section,  receiving  of  any  presents,  gifts  or 

other  benefits  by  an  officer  in  public  service,  whether  in  a  direct  or  indirect  form  shall  be  an 

offence fallen under Schedule One of the Establishments Code and further it has been stated that 

such act shall be a criminal offence for which a punishment of 07 years imprisonment and a fine 

up to Rs. 5000/- can be imposed under Section 19 (C) of the Bribery Act.  

 

03.  

There  may  be  instances  where  the  gift  parcels,  gift  vouchers,  air  tickets,  prepaid 

accommodation  facilities  at  hotels,  etc  are  offered  to  public  officers  by  the  private  companies 

during the festival seasons. Therefore, it is hereby emphasized to inform all the officers in your 

institution to refrain from receiving such gifts.
