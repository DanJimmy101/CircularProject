Amending Service Minutes, Schemes of Recruitment and Promotions in Public Service 

Your attention is drawn to the Public Administration Circular  25/2011 dated 16.11.2011 revised 

by Public Administration Circular  25/2011 (i) dated 14.03.2012.  

02.    Though  instructions  have  been  given  by  the  above  Circulars  to  amend  Service  Minutes, 

Schemes of Recruitments and Promotions in Public Service as per Public Administration Circular 

6/2006 and to obtain the approval of the Public Services Commission before 30.06.2012, it has 

been  reported  that  certain  authorities  have  further  postponed  to  act  in  accordance  with  the 

given instructions.   

03.    Therefore,  it  is  hereby  emphasized  that  you  are  required  to  take  immediate  actions  to 

amend the Service Minutes, Schemes of Recruitments and Promotions in Public Service as per 

Public  Administration  Circular  6/2006  and  to  obtain  the  approval  of  the  Public  Services 

Commission before 30.06.2012. Your personal attention in this regard is much appreciate.
