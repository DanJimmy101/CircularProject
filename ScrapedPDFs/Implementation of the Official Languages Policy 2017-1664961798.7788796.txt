Implementation of the Official Languages Policy 

Your  attention  is  drawn  to  para  02  of  Public  Administration  Circular  Letter  07/2014  issued  on 

21.01.2014 on the above matter.  

 

02. 

The said para is revised as follows.  

 

 

 

 

 

“It  is  hereby  notified  that,  the  officers  who  satisfy  the  requirement  of  obtaining 

proficiency  in  the  Other  Official  Languages  and  who  are  exempted  from  the 

requirement of obtaining proficiency in those languages by way of satisfying the other 

optional qualifications as per the provisions of Public Administration Circular 01/2014 

dated  21.01.2014  on  “Implementation  of  the  Official  Languages  Policy”  and 

consequent Circulars, may be informed in this regard once the Head of the respective 

Department  gets  it  confirmed  and  it  is  sufficient  to  forward  a  copy  of  same  to  the 

Administrative Authority in addition to the Appointing Authority for information.”
