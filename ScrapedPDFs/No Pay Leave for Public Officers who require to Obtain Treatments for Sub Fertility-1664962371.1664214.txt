   No Pay Leave for Public Officers who require to Obtain Treatments for 

Sub Fertility 

 

 
Your attention is drawn to sub section 22:9, Chapter XII of the Establishments Code which 
was revised by the Public Administration Circular 07/2017 dated 10.03.2017. 
 
02. 
revise the said Sub Section as follows. 

It has been decided at the meeting of the Cabinet of Ministers held on 14.12.2020 to 

 

“22:9  No pay leave for a maximum period of one year to be spent in or outside the island 

can be approved to female Public Officers for medical treatments on subfertility 

on  the  recommendation  of  Obstetrician  and  Gynecologist.  In  case  where 

Obstetrician and Gynecologist recommends medical treatments on subfertility for 

both husband and wife, leave to be spent continually in or outside the Island can 

be granted to male officers, subject to a maximum period of three months under 

Sub Section 22:1 and further no pay leave to be spent in or outside the island with 

or without break can also be granted subject to a maximum of 30 additional days 

if so required. ” 

 
03. 

 

These provisions shall be effective from 14.12.2020.
