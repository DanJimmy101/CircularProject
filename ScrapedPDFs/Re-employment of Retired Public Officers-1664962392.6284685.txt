Re-employment of Retired Public Officers 

 

Your  attention  is  drawn  to  the  Public  Administration  Circular  No:  03/2018  dated  20.02.2018, 
which includes the provisions relevant to re-employment of retired officers on contract basis. 
 
02. 
In  case  where  a  retired  public  officer  is  re-employed  on  contract  basis  as  per  the 
provisions  of  the  said  circular,  the  following  particulars  should  be  submitted  to  the  Director 
General of Pensions before the lapse of one month from the date of re-employment of the officer.  
 

I.  Name of the retired officer, who has been re-employed 

 

II.  The service to which the officer belonged and the post/ grade held by the officer before 

retirement 

 

III.  Post/ grade in which the officer has been re-employed and the service to which the post 

belongs 

 

IV.  Pension number  

 

V.  Letter by which approval has been received for re-employment 

 

VI.  Period for which approval has been received for re-employment 

Particulars  should  be  submitted  in  accordance  with  Para.  02  above  when  making 

 
03. 
appointment on contract basis and extending the period of such appointment of a retired officer.
