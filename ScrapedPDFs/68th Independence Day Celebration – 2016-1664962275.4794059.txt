68th Independence Day Celebration – 2016 

A decision has been made by the government  to  hoist the national  flag and illuminate all the 

buildings  where  the  government  offices  are  maintained  on  03rd  and  04th  of  February  2016  to 

mark the 68th Independence Day. 

 

02.  Therefore,  you  are  kindly  requested  to  inform  all  the  institutions  coming  under  your 

purview to take action accordingly.
