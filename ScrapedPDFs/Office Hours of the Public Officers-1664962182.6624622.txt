Office Hours of the Public Officers 

 

Your attention is drawn to Para 09 of the Public Administration Circular 162 dated 01.04.1980. 

 

02. 

In accordance with the provisions therein,  the office hours of the  Officers belonging to the 

Primary  Grade  is  8.00  a.m.  to  4.45  p.m.  including  the  30  minutes  lunch  break  as  per  the       

categorization of Public Administration Circular 06/2006 dated 25.04.2006. However, if no duties are 

to  be  performed  after  4.15  p.m.  the  Head  of  the  Department  shall  have  the  power  to  allow  such   

Officers  to  leave  the  office  and  the  time  from  8.00  a.m.  to  4.45  p.m.  shall  be  applied  to  calculate 

overtime. 

 

03. 

Further, no office time has been allocated to any service based on Grades and the provisions 

in the Public Administration Circular 09/2006 dated 30.05.2006 is further effective with regard to the 

office hours of the Public Officers.
