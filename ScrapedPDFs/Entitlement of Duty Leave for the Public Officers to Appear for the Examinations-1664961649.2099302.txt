Entitlement  of  Duty  Leave  for  the  Public  Officers  to  Appear  for  the    

Examinations for Acquiring Proficiency in Other Official Languages 

 

All  the  officers  who  are  recruited  on  open  or  limited  or  merit  basis  after  01.07.2007  shall 

acquire the level of proficiency in other official language prescribed for their posts within 05 

years from the date of appointment to the Public Service, in addition to the official language 

through  which  they  enter  the  service,  as  per  Public  Administration  Circular  07/2007  dated 

28.05.2007  and  Public  Administration  Circular  01/2014  dated  21.01.2014  issued  for  the 

implementation of official language policy.  

 

02.  

It has been reported that certain public institutions do not approve duty leave for the 

officers who appear for the examinations conducted by the Department of Official Languages 

or  the  Department  of  Examinations  for  the  purpose  of  acquiring  language  proficiency 

prescribed to be acquired as a Government policy. 

 

03.  

Therefore, duty leave shall be granted as per Public Administration Circular 07/2007 

and 01/2014 to Public Officers to appear for the examinations for acquiring proficiency in other 

official language, for the first sitting only, as per Section 19, Chapter XII of the Establishments 

Code.
