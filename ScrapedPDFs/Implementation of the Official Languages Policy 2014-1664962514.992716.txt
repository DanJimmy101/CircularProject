Implementation of the Official Languages Policy 

Your  attention  is  drawn  to the  Public Administration  Circular  01/2014  dated  21.01.2014 on 

the above matter.  

 

02. 

It  is  hereby  notified  that,  the  officers  who  satisfy  the  requirement  of  obtaining 

proficiency  in  the  Other  Official  Language  and  who  are  exempted  from  the  requirement of 

obtaining proficiency in those languages and other optional qualifications as per said Circular 

and consequent Circulars  instructions,  may  be  informed  in this regard once the Head of the 

respective Department gets it confirmed and it is sufficient to forward a copy of same to the 

Appointing Authority for information.
