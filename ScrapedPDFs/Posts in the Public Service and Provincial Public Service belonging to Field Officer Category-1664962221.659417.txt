Posts in the Public Service and Provincial Public Service belonging to  

Field Officer Category  

 
All  the officers serving in the posts mentioned in  Annex 01 or  02 of Public Administration 
Circular 33/2017 dated 07.12.2017 are entitled to the allowance for the field officers as per the 
provisions  in  the  Public  Administration  Circular  06/2014  dated  31.03.2014,  Public 
Administration Circular 06/2014(I) dated 30.06.2014 and the Public Administration Circular 
06/2014(II) dated 03.06.2015.  
 
02. 
This  circular  letter  is  issued  considering  the  representations  made  stating  that  the 
allowances for certain filed officers are not paid in line with the provisions of the Circulars. 
Hence it is further informed that if there is any issue with regard to the payment of allowance 
to any officer, relevant head of the institution should forward the issue to the Director General 
of Establishments and obtain clarifications.
