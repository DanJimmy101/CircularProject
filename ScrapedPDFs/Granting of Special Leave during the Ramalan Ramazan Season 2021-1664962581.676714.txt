Granting of Special Leave during the Ramalan (Ramazan) Season 2021 

 

 

As the forthcoming Ramalan (Ramazan) Season is due to commence on 13 April 2021 and end 
on 14 May 2021 you are requested to make arrangements to enable Muslim Officers to take 
part in prayers and religious activities during this period. 
 
02. 
follows: 

The times during which such prayers and religious activities will take place daily are as 

*  03.30  a.m. to  06.00  a.m. 
*  03.15  p.m. to  04.15  p.m. 
*  06.00  p.m. to  07.00  p.m. 
*  07.30  p.m. to  10.30  p.m. 

 

03.  Work shifts may be arranged wherever possible to enable those officers to take part in 
religious  activities during these periods. Only in  case of unavoidable circumstances, special 
leave may be approved. 
 
04. 
Please  take  action  to  grant  the  Festival  Advance  to  eligible  Muslim  Officers  in  the 
Public Service, Public Corporations and Statutory  Boards 14 days before the expiry date of 
Ramalan (Ramazan).
