Declaring 02 Special Holidays for Government Offices 

 

This should not hinder the provision of essential government services. 

I hereby declare 24 and 25 of May 2021 as two special holidays for government offices, to make the 
efforts of the government to minimize the movement in the country a success, in order to minimize 
the rapid spread of Covid 19.
