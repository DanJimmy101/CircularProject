Granting a monthly allowance of Rs. 5000/- to Public Officers and Pensioners 
 
A  decision  has  been  taken  at  the  meeting  of  the  Cabinet  of  Ministers  held  on  03.01.2022  to 
grant  a monthly  allowance of Rs.5000/- which is not  taken into account  for the  calculation of 
pension, to all Public Officers and Pensioners with effect from 01.01.2022.
