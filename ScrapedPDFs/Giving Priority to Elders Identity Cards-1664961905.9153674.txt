Giving Priority to Elders' Identity Cards. 

 
Sri  Lanka  is  a  country  in  the  South  Asian  region  where  the  population  of  elders  is 
growing rapidly and at present the population of elders is 10% of the total  population. 
 
02. Arrangements have been made by the Ministry of Social Services and Social Welfare, 
to issue the elders' identity cards, through the Divisional Secretariats since 1999 to-date 
with  the  object  of  giving  priority  to  the  elders  in  providing  their  various  service 
requirements. 
 
03. I would like to request you to pay attention to the following matters when providing 
services to these elders who are over 60 years of age. 
 

I.  Giving priority to the holders of elders' identity cards when providing services by 

II.  Arranging the offices to enable elders to obtain services easily.
