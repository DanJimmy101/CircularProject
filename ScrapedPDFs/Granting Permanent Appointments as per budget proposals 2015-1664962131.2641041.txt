Granting Permanent Appointments as per budget proposals 2015 to the employees 

recruited on Temporary, Casual (on daily wages), Substitute, Contract or relief Basis 

 

Your attention is drawn to para 03 of Public Administration Circular 25/2014 dated 12.11.2014 on 

the above matter.  

 

02. 

As  per  the provisions  in  the  said  para  when  the  employees,  who  have been  recruited  to 

service  categories  mentioned  under  (d),  (e),  (f)  and  (g)  on  temporary,  casual  (on  daily  wages), 

substitute,  contract  or  relief  basis,  are  confirmed  in  the  posts  they  should  have  passed  G.C.E. 

(O/L) in at least six(06) subjects with Language or Literature and Mathematics at one sitting. In 

this  regard,  passing  only  the  subject  Mathematics  at  not  more  than  two  sittings  can  be  made 

applicable. 

 

03. 

This circular letter is issued on the concurrence of the Treasury.
