Payment of Claims under Agrahara Insurance Scheme for Public Officers 

tested Positive for COVID - 19 

 
Inquiries have been made by the Heads of the Institutions as well as Public Officers from this 
Ministry  on  the  benefits  entitled  to  the  Public  Officers,  who  are  tested  positive  for       
COVID -19, under the Agrahara Insurance Scheme. As per the inquiry made from National 
Insurance Trust Fund in this regard, it has been informed that action will be taken to pay the 
claims  under  the  Agrahara  Insurance  Scheme  for  officers,  who  receive  treatments  for  
COVID - 19, in considering the way they receive treatments. 
 
02. 
Accordingly,  all  the  provisions  including  the  claims  that  will  be  paid,  methodology 
for  payment  of  claims  and  documents  to  be  submitted  for  this  purpose  can  be  obtained 
through the official website of National Trust Fund (www.nitf.lk).
