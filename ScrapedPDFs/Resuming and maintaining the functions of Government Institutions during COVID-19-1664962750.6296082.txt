Resuming and maintaining the functions of Government Institutions during 

COVID-19 

 
Your  attention  is  hereby  drawn  to  the  Public  Administration  Circular  Letter  01/2020  dated 
29.04.2020 issued on the above matter.   
 
02. 
Having  taken  into  consideration  the  facts  pointed  out  by  the  Authorities  of  Health 
Sector  in  respect  of  using  disinfection  cubicles  to  prevent  the  spread  of  COVID-19,  it  is 
hereby informed that the establishment of such disinfection cubicles within the institutions is 
not  necessary  and  in  case  where  disinfection  cubicles  are  to  be  established  depending  on 
exceptional  circumstances,  action  should  be  taken  to  seek  proper  advices  from  Health 
Authorities.
