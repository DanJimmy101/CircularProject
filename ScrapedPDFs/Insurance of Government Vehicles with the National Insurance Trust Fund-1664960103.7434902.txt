Your attention is drawn to the Public Finance Circular PF/427 dated 18.12.2007, PF/427 (1) dated 

13.03.2008  issued  by  the  Secretary of  the  General Treasury  on  the  above matter and  the  Public 

Finance  Circular  PF/437  dated  18.09.2009  on  “Provision  of  General  Insurance  Cover  for 

Government Institutions”.  

 

2. 

As per the provisions of the said circulars the motor vehicles of all government and semi 

government  institutions  should  be  insured  by  the  National  Insurance  Trust  Fund  or  Sri  Lanka 

Insurance Corporations Ltd on the requirement of each institution. 

 

3. 

However, it has been observed that issues are arisen at the time of reimbursement of the 

compensation  for  vehicle  accidents  due  to  not  insuring  the  motor  vehicles  belonging  to 

government institutions as per the above instructions.  

 

4. 

Therefore, you are requested to take actions to insure the motor vehicles belonging to your 

institution as per the provisions of the Public Finance Circulars mentioned in para 01 above and  

if  you  require  further  clarifications  in  this  regard  you  are  informed  to  inquire  the  Additional 

Director General of Department of Public Finance as per the provisions in para 06 of the Public 

Finance Circular PF/ 437.  

 

5. 

 

 

 
 
 

This Circular has been issued with the concurrence of the General Treasury.
