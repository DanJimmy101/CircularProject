Indicating the Number of the National Identity Cards in the Loan Application Forms  

 

Your attention is drawn to Sub Section 3:13 of Chapter XXIV of the Establishments Code.  

 

02. 

 According to the provisions in the said section, it is hereby informed that when loan application forms 

are  prepared  by  the  authority  for  granting  loans,  the  number  of  the  National  Identity  Card  should  be 

compulsorily indicated in the forms.
