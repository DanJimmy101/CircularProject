Reference the course related to the exemption of the officers in Grade I of 

Management Service Officers’ Service from the Efficiency Bar Examination 

 
As  per  the  Minute  of  the  Management  Service  Officers’  Service  published  in  the  Gazette 
Extraordinary No. 1840/34 dated 11.12.2013, the officers of the said service are required to pass 
the  Efficiency  Bar  Examination  prescribed  for  Grade  I  within  five  years  from  the  date  of 
appointment to the said Grade. 
 
02.   Accordingly,  you  are  kindly  informed  that  it  has  been  informed  by  the  letter  of  the 
Secretary of the Public Service Commission No. PSC/APP/4/62/2018 dated 08.11.2021 that the 
said Commission has decided to  extend the concessionary period granted up to 01.01.2021 by 
the Combined Services Circular Letter No. 02/2019 for the officers who have already completed 
a service period of five years in Grade I but have been deprived of the salary increments due to 
not being able to complete the Efficiency Bar prescribed for Grade I, up to 31.12.2022.
