Preparation of Transfer Schemes  for all Services in Public Sector 

 

Your attention is drawn to the Public Administration Circular  21/2011 dated 25.10.2011 on the above 

subject.  

 

02.    Though  instructions  have  been  issued  by  the  above  Circular  to  Appointing/  Administrative 

Authorities  who  are  in  charge  of  the  relevant  services,  to  prepare  transfer  schemes  urgently  in 

accordance with the decision taken by the Cabinet of Ministers on 17.08.2011, it has been reported that 

transfer procedures have not yet been prepared.  

 

03.  Accordingly it is hereby requested to take immediate actions to prepare transfer schemes for all the 

services  in the public  sector inclusive all island  services  for  which the transfer schemes have not been 

prepared so far, and to obtain the approval of the Public Services Commission. Your personal attention 

in this regard is solicited.
