Attendance of the Officers in the Public Service on 20.05.2022  

 
As stated by the Hon. Prime Minister in Parliament on 19.05.2022, in view of the current fuel 
shortage  and  issues  in  transport  facilities  in  the  country,  it  is  hereby  informed  that  public 
officers,  except  those  who  are  required  to  maintain  essential  services,  are  not  required  to 
report for duty on 20.05.2022.
